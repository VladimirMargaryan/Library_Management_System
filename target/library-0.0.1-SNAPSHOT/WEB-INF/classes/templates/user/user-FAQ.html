FAQ

    Can't find your answer here? Please reach out to us by sending an email to customerservice@library.com.

    For how long can I borrow a book?

    Books can be borrowed for 15 days. This period can be extended by 15 days. Extending is free of charge and can only be done if no one else made a reservation for that particular book. The status of your books can be checked on your personal library account on our website.

    How can I reserve a book?

    Search for the book via your personal library account on our website and click the "Reserve book" button. A reservations are valid for 7 days. If the book is not picked-up after 7 days, the reservation will automatically be canceled.

    What happens if I can't return a book on time?

    In case you can't return a book you will be blocked by system and will receive an email about that and about your next steps what to do.

    What happens if I lose a book?

    Please contact our customer service as soon as possible. The library will charge a small price for the book, depending on its value.
